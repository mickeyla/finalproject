About Focal Focus

Procrastination is a problem that hinders many people including a vast amount of students. To help with this problem, Focal Focus was made FOR students BY students. Focal Focus is an interactive website that is supposed to be interactive, helpful, and organized; all through promoting better time management and healthy studying habits.

About The Creators

The creators, Alexis Denny and Mickeyla Clark, are graduating Girls Who Code students and procrastinators. Their goal is to help students like them be able to manage their time.
