About Focal Focus

Procrastination is a problem that hinders many people including a vast amount of students. To help with this problem, Focal Focus was made FOR students BY students. Focal Focus is an interactive website that is supposed to be interactive, helpful, and organized; all through promoting better time management and healthy studying habits.

About the Creators

Overall

The creators of Focal Focus were participants in the 2018 Girls Who Code summer program. Girls Who Code is a program that was founded by Reshma Saujani. Her goal was to get more young women at least interested in the computer science field and to promote sisterhood. They spent their summer at the Georgetown Law School, which was the site for BSA Software.org. They attended trips to Congress, the Capital, and other tech related places like AT&T. Through their seven week long intership, they covered beginner code, basic code, robotics, web developement, and data. During the summer, they learned various progamming languages such as Scratch, Python, Arduino, HTML, CSS, and Java.
